Welcome

Please take a look around.

I'm a mother of two, full time liability analyst for auto claims with Liberty Mutual and an avid Disney Parks lover.

My career started while working for Walt Disney World on a college internship, the Disney College Program. My first go round I was a snack vendor at Disney's Hollywood Studios. After finishing my Hospitality and Tourism degree at Purdue University I went back to be a theater hostess at Disney's Animal Kingdom.

Shortly after moving to the sunshine state, my husband and I moved back to Indiana to start a family. I worked in retail for a couple of years as a shift manager at Walgreens before making the switch into insurance.

I have been working for Liberty Mutual for nearly 7 years. I started in the contact center and earned a promotion to a liability investigator in auto claims. Daily, I handle claims for people in the midwest who have been involved in an accident or have other damage to their vehicles.

I'm a very detail oriented and organized person. I am focused, driven and dedicated. I enjoy logic puzzles and problem solving. I feel as if software development will help me use these passions and skills to help solve problems and make lives easier.
